eCommerce Fulfillment Service Center in Canada

Delight your customers with fast & cheap 1-3 days delivery across Canada, and cheaper international shipments. Send us your items to one or more of our warehouses across Canada, and we will handle shipping for you. .


3PL Fulfillment Services

  1. We fulfill your eCommerce orders (Shopify, Amazon FBM, WooCommerce, Wix etc.) for a fee of CAD$4.50 per order.
  2. Same-day shipping on orders placed by 3 PM local warehouse time
  3. replenish Amazon FBA stock. (fee: hourly wage)
  4. Pallet storage. (CAD$25/pallet/month)

Connect Your Own Carriers

On top of our re-sellers with carriers, you can connect your own carriers such as UPS, Canada Post, Canpar, and ICS.

CASHBACK

Cashback for delayed or missing / lost-in-transit items. If the carrier loses or delays your transit items, we will work for you to get your money back. Note: the pandemic may affect the availability of this service.
